The goal of this game is to elimnate the squares by clicking them to gain 1 point

Each square requires multiple clicks depending on your luck and the square's size

1. Click on Start start the countdown

2. Click on Add Counter to add squares

3. Click on the squares to try to eliminate them

4. Add more square by clicking on Add Counter if there are in sufficient squares
